<dino-loader>

The real Chrome “T-Rex Runner” game (original Chromium code, BSD license), looping forever and non-playable: it starts on its own, the dino runs and auto-jumps over the cacti, and the loop never stops. Size adapts via height; speed sets the pace; label adds a caption; color recolors the scene; dark inverts the dino for dark backgrounds.
